Reviving Art

Unearth the indigenous type forms scattered across the breathtaking valleys of Kashmir. Every hidden gem tells a story, and our ambitious community has taken it upon itself to preserve them for future generations.

From the alleys of Srinagar to the wooden doors of old havelis, type forms come to life in every nook and corner. Explore the perfect fusion of aesthetics, culture, and history as you navigate through this typographic wonderland.

What started as lone wanderers documenting type forms, has now transformed into a thriving community with a shared passion for design, culture, and preservation.

Get Involved

Can’t resist the alluring beauty of type forms? Awaken your inner explorer, and dive into the enchanting world of Kashmir’s indigenous typography. Submit your discoveries and join our passionate community in the quest for preserving our heritage.
